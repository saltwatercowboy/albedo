album covers, dithering tools
10 05 26

Took the time to look through some of my old SD cards over the weekend.

They're mostly full of loose and ephemeral images: the spire of the Holy Trinity Church (on E 88th & 1st), the view from the window above my old writing desk, a photo of an alarmingly fat dog. Pointless moments I knew I'd like to remember for no reason at all.
I tweaked these a little in lightroom, then stylized them into track/album covers with the dithering toolkit I've been creating (music in refs).

Next up? Extend the toolkit and tackle pixel sorting. There are tons of little visual techniques I think need to be in the same place.
